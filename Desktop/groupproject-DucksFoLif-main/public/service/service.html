FAQ

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Yes, we offer a full 30-day refund for any item purchased. Please keep your receipt as proof of purchase.
Q: Do you sell the latest card sets?
A: No, we often do not sell the latest sets. We focus on older and more collectible card sets.
Q: Are all products officially licensed by Pokémon?
A: Yes, all items we sell are officially licensed by The Pokémon Company.
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Shipping typically takes 5–7 business days within the United States. International shipping may take 10–14 business days depending on the destination.
Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location.
Q: Do you buy Pokémon cards from collectors?
A: Yes, we occasionally buy cards from collectors. Please contact us with details and photos of the cards you want to sell.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: Items must be returned within 30 days in their original condition to be eligible for a refund or exchange. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the buyer.
Q: Do you authenticate rare cards?
A: Yes, we offer card authentication services for an additional fee. Contact us for more details.
Q: Can I preorder upcoming card sets?
A: No, we do not currently offer preorders for upcoming sets.
Q: Are the mystery packs worth the price?
A: Mystery packs offer great value, especially for collectors looking to add variety to their collection. There's also a chance to receive rare and valuable cards.
Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can contact us via the contact form on this page or email us directly at support@justinbrower.dev.
Q: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we are unable to make changes. Please review your order carefully before finalizing your purchase.
Q: How can I track my order?
A: You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been shipped. You can use this to track your package online.
Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, we use SSL encryption to ensure your payment details are secure. Your privacy is our top priority.
Q: Do you offer gift cards?
A: Yes, we offer digital gift cards in various denominations. You can purchase them on our website.
Q: What should I do if I receive a damaged item?
A: If you receive a damaged item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the product. We’ll arrange a replacement or refund.
Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase. After that, we are unable to cancel orders, as they may have already been processed or shipped.
Q: Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?
A: Yes, we offer discounts on bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for more details and pricing.
Q: Can I use multiple discount codes on one order?
A: Unfortunately, only one discount code can be used per order.
Q: Do you accept exchanges?
A: Yes, we accept exchanges within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in unused, original condition.
Q: Can I purchase from your website without creating an account?
A: Yes, you can complete a purchase as a guest. However, creating an account will allow you to track orders and save your information for future purchases.
Q: How do I contact customer service?
A: You can reach our customer service team by emailing support@ourwebsite.com or calling 1-800-123-4567.
Q: Are your products covered by a warranty?
A: Yes, many of our products come with a one-year warranty. Please refer to the product details or contact customer service for specific warranty information.
Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the continental United States.
Q: Can I modify my shipping address after placing an order?
A: If your order hasn't been shipped yet, you can modify your shipping address by contacting our customer support team. Once shipped, we are unable to change the address.
Q: How do I redeem a discount code?
A: To redeem a discount code, simply enter the code at checkout in the "Promo Code" box and click "Apply."
Q: What if I forgot to apply my discount code?
A: Unfortunately, we cannot apply a discount code to an order after it has been completed. Please double-check your code before finalizing the checkout process.
Q: Do you offer price matching?
A: We do not offer price matching at this time, but we frequently run sales and promotions. Be sure to sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest offers.
Q: How can I unsubscribe from your newsletter?
A: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send you.
