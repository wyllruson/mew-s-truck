FAQ

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Yes, we offer a full 30-day refund for any item purchased. Please keep your receipt as proof of purchase.
Q: Do you sell the latest card sets?
A: No, we often do not sell the latest sets. We focus on older and more collectible card sets.
Q: Are all products officially licensed by Pokémon?
A: Yes, all items we sell are officially licensed by The Pokémon Company.
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Shipping typically takes 5–7 business days within the United States.
Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location.
Q: Do you buy Pokémon cards from collectors?
A: Yes, we occasionally buy cards from collectors. Please contact us with details and photos.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: Items must be returned within 30 days in their original condition to be eligible for a refund or exchange.
Q: Do you authenticate rare cards?
A: Yes, we offer card authentication services for an additional fee. Contact us for more details.
Q: Can I preorder upcoming card sets?
A: No, we do not currently offer preorders for upcoming sets.
Q: Are the mystery packs worth the price?
A: Mystery packs offer great value, especially for collectors looking to add variety to their collection.
Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can contact us via the contact form on this page or email support@justinbrower.dev.
Q: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we are unable to make changes.
Q: How can I track my order?
A: You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been shipped.
Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, we use SSL encryption to ensure your payment details are secure.
Q: Do you offer gift cards?
A: Yes, we offer digital gift cards in various denominations on our website.
Q: What should I do if I receive a damaged item?
A: Contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the product.
Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase.
Q: Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?
A: Yes, we offer discounts on bulk orders. Please contact our sales team.
Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the continental United States.
